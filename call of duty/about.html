ABOUT CALL OF DUTY

One of the world's most popular action game franchises.

The Franchise

Call of Duty is a famous first-person shooter game series known for its exciting campaigns, multiplayer battles, and memorable characters. Over the years, the franchise has explored historical wars, modern conflicts, and futuristic settings.

Players can experience story-driven missions, cooperative gameplay, and competitive online matches across various game modes.
